This dataset provides comprehensive genre statistics and revenue data for movies released between 1995 and 2018, allowing for analysis of trends, profitability assessment, and comparative analysis of different film genres

- Genre: The category or type of movie. (Categorical)
- Year: The year in which the movies were released. (Numeric)
- Movies Released: The number of movies released in that particular genre and year. (Numeric)
- Gross: The total gross revenue generated by movies in that genre and year. (Numeric)
- Tickets Sold: The total number of tickets sold for movies in that genre and year. (Numeric)
- Inflation-Adjusted Gross: The gross revenue adjusted for inflation, considering changes in the value of money over time. (Numeric)
- Top Movie: The title of the highest-grossing movie in that genre and year. (Text)
- Top Movie Gross (That Year): The gross revenue generated by the highest-grossing movie in that genre and year. (Numeric)
- Top Movie Inflation-Adjusted Gross (That Year): The gross revenue of the highest-grossing movie adjusted for inflation in that genre and year. (Numeric)

Dataset link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/thedevastator/film-genre-statistics
